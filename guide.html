Getting Started in Survival Mode

When you first start a new world, your main goal is to survive the first night. You will need to gather resources quickly before the sun goes down and monsters appear.

Essential First Steps

Follow these steps to ensure you have a safe place to stay and the tools necessary to progress in the game.

  1. Punch a tree to collect wood logs.
  2. Open your inventory to craft wooden planks.
  3. Build a crafting table to make better tools.
  4. Find coal or craft charcoal to make torches for light.

Important Resources and Tools

As you explore deeper into the world, you will find different materials. Each material has its own durability and strength levels for tools and armor.

Material Type Primary Use
Wood Basic tools and building blocks.
Iron Stronger armor and mid-tier tools.
Diamond High-durability gear and obsidian mining.

Items You Should Always Carry

  • A stack of food to heal your hunger bar.
  • A sword or bow for defending against mobs.
  • A pickaxe for mining stone and ores.
  • Torches to light up dark caves and prevent monster spawns.
